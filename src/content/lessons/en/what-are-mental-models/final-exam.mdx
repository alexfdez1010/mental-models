<Callout variant="success" title="Key takeaways">
A mental model is a useful simplification of how something works. Every model is
less than the reality it describes, so stay alert to what it leaves out. No single
model is enough — a latticework of models from many fields covers each other's
blind spots. And all of it rests on honesty about your circle of competence: the
edge of what you genuinely understand. Carry these four, and you're ready for the
next models on the ladder.
</Callout>
