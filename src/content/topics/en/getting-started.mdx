Welcome 👋 This course exists so the template **builds and runs out of the box** —
and so you can see exactly how a *topic*, its *lessons*, and the reusable
interactive components fit together before you write your own.

Here's the arc:

- **Welcome & how this works** — what a topic is, how lessons are ordered into a
  zero-to-expert ladder, and where everything lives.
- **The interactive toolkit** — a hands-on tour of the components you drop into
  any lesson: callouts, multiple-choice questions, quizzes, fill-in-the-blank,
  reveals, and mind maps.

When you adopt the template (run the **`bootstrap-topic`** skill), this example
gets replaced with your subject. Until then, poke around — every page, the
catalog graph, the OG images, and the bilingual routing all work right now.
